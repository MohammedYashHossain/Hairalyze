Frequently Asked Questions

What is Hairalyze?
Hairalyze is an AI-powered tool that scans your hair from a photo and gives you real feedback—like how frizzy, oily, dry, or damaged it is.
Is Hairalyze free to use?
Hairalyze offers a free version with essential features, and will introduce premium options as development continues.
What do I need to get started?
Just take a photo of your hair, and Hairalyze will analyze it instantly. You'll receive personalized ratings and tips.
