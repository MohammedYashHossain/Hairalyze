Frequently Asked Questions

What is Hairalyze?
Hairalyze is an AI-powered tool that scans your hair from a photo and gives you real feedback—like how frizzy, oily, dry, or damaged it is. It helps you understand your hair's condition and suggests how to care for it more effectively.
Is Hairalyze free to use?
Hairalyze offers a free version with essential features, and will introduce premium options with enhanced insights as development continues.
What do I need to get started?
Just take a photo of your hair, and Hairalyze will analyze it instantly. You'll receive personalized ratings, see trends over time, and even chat with your AI stylist, Curlina, for tailored advice.
